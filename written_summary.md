Written Summary

 When sunlight, solar array, online battery, battery capacity, and a completed tick are all avaliable, the CLI calculates generated energy and adds it to the battery without exceeding its maximum capacity.

The small solar array generates power using its generation attribute, the server-provided sunlight multiplier, and a 50 percent efficiency value. Lower irradiance creates less energy. No irradiance creates no energy. Offline modules or a full battery prevent charging.
